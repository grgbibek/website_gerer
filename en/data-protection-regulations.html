Data Protection Regulations

These data protection regulations apply for the SpyOFF product produced by Sareta S.r.l.

Personal data is subject to special legal protection. In order that this protection is as secure as possible, the following data protection regulations issued by Sareta S.r.l. are intended to explain the purposes for which we collect, process and use data.

If you still have any unanswered questions regarding the handling of personal data, please contact the responsible body:

SpyOFF is a product offered by Sareta S.r.l., Serravalle (R.S.M.) 10 Cardio Street, 47899 Serravalle, Repubblica San Marino, Email: support@spyoff.com.

 

1. Data collection

Should you decide to register with SpyOFF, you will be asked to provide contact details as a means of identification. In addition, you will also be requested to provide payment details, which will enable the processing of payment in order to fulfil the mutually arising contractual obligations. Furthermore, we may also use your contact details to inform you of new products, improvements and changes to our service.

This data includes your first name and surname, your address details, telephone number and payment details, as well as a valid email. Individual details can of course be corrected if your circumstances change over the course of time. Your data will be saved for as long as your account with SpyOFF exists and the data is required to be available within the scope of legal obligations to retain records or the obligations under the contractual relationship. If it is no longer necessary to store the data and the legal obligations to retain records are terminated, they will be deleted.

As soon as you call up the SpyOFF website, we send one or more so-called cookies to your computer. The purpose of the use of such cookies is to tailor the Sareta S.r.l. website to your requirements as effectively as possible. A cookie is a small text file that contains a certain sequence of characters and uniquely identifies your browser. The use of cookies allows us to let you log into the customer area and to trace whether or not you have followed a specific link. You can prevent the use of cookies by applying the appropriate setting in your browser software. However, in this case you will not be able to use all of the functions of this website. Furthermore, when calling up the web pages, Sareta S.r.l. stores the URL referrer, your IP address and the date and time that the page was called up.

 

2. Use of data within the scope of the purpose of the agreement and in certain other cases

Sareta S.r.l. uses personal data for the purposes outlined below:

  1. a) The use of the services offered by Sareta S.r.l., as well as email advertising provided that the user has issued consent.
  2. b) The continual further development and safeguarding of our offering, as well as the effective management of our services.

As a rule, your personal data will only be collected, stored, processed and used for the described purposes. If the desired purpose has been achieved, your data will be deleted. The only instance whereby the deletion will not take place is if and to the extent that there is an obligation to retain the data under statutory provisions.

Recipients to whom the data may be sent for the purpose of the fulfilment of customer contracts are the external service providers. Otherwise, we only forward data that can be traced to you personally to other third parties, without your express consent, if there exists a legal obligation to do so. Statutory provisions may, under certain conditions, require us to forward personal data to investigating authorities.

 

3. Security and responsibility

Data that is stored on our servers is protected by means of the continual updating of our data security software. Particularly sensitive data will be transferred with a high standard of security (TLS technology).

Only the respective acting bodies are responsible for the collection, processing and use of personal data. Accordingly, Sareta S.r.l. is not responsible for adherence to data protection regulations on the part of these third parties.

 

4. Amendment and deletion of your personal data

If you have already registered with SpyOFF, you can update your name and address details and the settings regarding the receipt of newsletters yourself in the member area. For security reasons, it is not possible to amend payment details. If there have been changes to this data of which you would like to inform us, you can do this via the email support.

6. Google AdWords and Google Display Network

Our web page uses the online advertising systems Google AdWords and Google Display Network. Google affirms that it will protect the data of its customers and users. Google Adwords installs a cookie on your computer here. These cookies become invalid after 30 days and are not used for personal identification. If the user visits certain pages of the web page of the Adwords customer and the cookie has not yet expired, Google and the customer can see that the user clicked on the advertisement and was forwarded to this page. The information obtained as a result is used to draw up statistics, which are assessed in anonymised form. We also use these Google Adwords functions to speak to visitors of this web page again on web pages of third party suppliers or Internet users with specific interest profiles that are based on their Internet use. We do not collect any personal information here. This assessment is carried out in order to account for and evaluate advertisements and contains no personal data on you. Users who do not wish to take part in tracking can easily deactivate the Google Conversion Tracking cookie on their Internet browser under User Settings. These users are not included in the conversion tracking statistics.

We should point out that Google has its own data protection regulations. We assume no responsibility or liability for these regulations and processes. Please read Google‚Äôs data protection regulations before using our web page.

We reserve the right to amend these data protection regulations at any time. If amendments are merely insignificant, they shall come into force immediately. Should significant sections be amended, we will inform you by email one month before the amendment comes into force.
